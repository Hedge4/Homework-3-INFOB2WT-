About Us

Thanks for visiting our fake nonexisting theater! Running a fake theater may seem like a piece of cake, but believe me, it's not all fun and games and can be tough work sometimes. Here are just a few of the things we have to deal with:

  • We have to come up with new movie titles that sound believable, yet aren't real.
  • We have to make sure our "sold out" signs look realistic enough so people don't catch on or try to buy a ticket.
  • Earning money is harder than it seems if you don't show any movies.
  • And don't even get us started on the popcorn situation...

Despite these challenges, we love what we do and we hope you'll enjoy your non-movie-going experience with us. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
